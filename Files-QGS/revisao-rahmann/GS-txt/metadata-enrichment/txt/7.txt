Automated Provisioning of Application in IAAS Cloud using Ansible Configuration Management. Cloud has become a dominant technology in todays information technology environment and the need arises to abide with the growing demand of clients. The pressure on information technology organizations is constantly increasing to deploy the customers application over private cloud. This change has come into light since a large number of clients have directly started to contact the cloud venders for this support. Moreover, the DevOps teams are in much bigger focus now since they are responsible for the automation and provisioning of the whole environment along with the client application. This paper focuses upon the automation of customer application right from environment provisioning to application deployment. In this paper the whole architecture of automated application deployment assembled using amazon as IAAS provider and Ansible as the orchestration engine.
